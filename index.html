Coaches have an almost impossible task to scout potential players who arrive at their team tryouts. Even with multiple coaches, the head coach can't be at all places on the field at once, whether that be a hitting or fielding station. Often times, a prospect is not given proper attention as there is not a way for the head coach to see each player's unique performances. The Ultimate Tryout tracker attempts to solve this problem by becoming a data aggregation and storage tool for all live stations. This accrued data is available later for the coaching staff to review and analyze, free from other distractions. This app's goal is to present the most accurate portrayal of a player's talent.

The initial build of the Ultimate Tryout Tracker will be geared toward baseball but in the development of the app it is our goal to make it as modular as possible in order to facilitate the ability to swap in different sports. Whatever sport is selected by the end user, the app will present ratings scales and player evaluation verbiage particular to the sport. The main sports which could benefit from this type of granular player tracking are soccer, football, and basketball.
